Our Treatments

Curated therapies designed to alleviate modern stress and chronic pain, focusing on holistic well-being.

Swedish Relaxation

A gentle, flowing massage that uses long strokes to improve circulation, ease muscle tension, and promote a deep sense of calm. Perfect for stress relief and first-time massage clients.

Deep Tissue Therapy

Focuses on realigning deeper layers of muscles and connective tissue. Highly effective for chronic aches, stiff necks, low back tightness, and sore shoulders from office work.

Foot Reflexology

Based on the principle that points on the feet correspond to organs and systems of the body. Pressure is applied to restore energy flow and balance the entire body.

Aromatherapy SPA

A holistic experience combining soft massage with therapeutic essential oils to boost mood, reduce anxiety, and improve sleep quality. A truly sensory journey.

Common Questions

When should I arrive?

Please arrive 10-15 minutes before your scheduled appointment time. This allows you to fill out a health intake form, use the restroom, and begin relaxing before your session starts.

Is there parking available?

Yes, we have a dedicated parking lot for customers right in front of the store. It is free of charge for the duration of your visit.

Can I get a massage if I have an injury?

It depends on the injury. Please inform us when booking and consult your doctor. In many cases, our therapists can work *around* an injury to provide relief, but we need to know in advance.

What is your cancellation policy?

We require 24 hours notice for cancellations. Cancellations made less than 24 hours in advance may be subject to a 50% cancellation fee.
